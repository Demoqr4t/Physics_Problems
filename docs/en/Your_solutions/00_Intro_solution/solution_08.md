# Definite Integral Calculation

## Problem Statement
Calculate the area under the curve of the function $f(x) = \sin(x)$ from $x=0$ to $x=\pi$.

## Necessary Formulas

$$
\int_{a}^{b} f(x) \, dx = F(b) - F(a)
$$

$$
\int \sin(x) \, dx = -\cos(x) + C
$$

## Solution

1. **Setup:**
   
$$
A = \int_{0}^{\pi} \sin(x) \, dx
$$

2. **Antiderivative:**
   
$$
A = [-\cos(x)]_{0}^{\pi}
$$

3. **Evaluation:**
   
$$
A = (-\cos(\pi)) - (-\cos(0))
$$

$$
A = (-(-1)) - (-1)
$$

4. **Final Result:**
   
$$
A = 1 + 1 = 2
$$
